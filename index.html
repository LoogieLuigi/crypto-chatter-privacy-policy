Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

CryptoChatter ("we", "our", "us") operates the CryptoChatter mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of the App.

2. Information We Collect

Email Address: We collect your email address when you create an account on CryptoChatter. This information is necessary for authentication and to provide you access to the App.

Username: We collect the username you create to identify your account within the App.

App Activity: We collect information about your app activity, including crash logs and stack traces, to improve the stability and performance of the App.

Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the App, such as the device ID and type.

3. How We Use Your Information

Authentication: Your email address and username are used to authenticate your account and allow you to access the App.

Communication: We may use your email address to communicate important updates about the App, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.

Improvement: App activity information, including crash logs and stack traces, is used to diagnose issues, improve the App’s stability, and enhance performance.

Device Information: We use device information to optimize the App’s performance and ensure compatibility with different devices.

4. Data Sharing

Firebase: We use Firebase to store your email address and username securely. Firebase is a service provided by Google LLC, and its privacy policy can be found at Firebase Privacy Policy.

Crashlytics: We use Crashlytics, a crash reporting tool provided by Firebase, to collect crash logs and stack traces. The information collected helps us diagnose and fix issues within the App. Crashlytics’ privacy policy can be found at Crashlytics Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your personal information. We use various security measures, including encryption, to ensure the safety of your data.

6. Data Retention

Your email address, username, and device information are retained as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, this data and associated information will be deleted from our systems. Crash logs and stack traces are retained for a reasonable period to analyze and fix issues.

7. User Rights

Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information.

Deletion: You have the right to delete your account and associated data. This can be done through the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cryptochatter.com.
